UNAIDS HIVOS and NAYA asked us to build an informative narration game prototype on HIV prevention and treatment among young women in Nairobi, Kenya.

To create a sensible proposition we worked in close collaboration with local activists. As it is a complex subject our first objective was to not misrepresent the population and the matter in an insensitive way. It was also an ethnographic challenge to understand the culture and the local struggle that young women face daily.

Our proposition was a detective game, in which you play as a teenager unraveling the death of her older sister. During the experience, you will learn about HIV, sexuality, and come to terms with your family by interacting with in-game characters.
