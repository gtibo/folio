This edition is the result of questions raised by the medium of generative drawing. It explores the idea of repetition, inspired by the works of Vera Molnar and alike.

Is the drawing generated from a formula the same when made by a machine or a human? What's our choice in front of the infinity and the abundance of variations proposed by the machine, and what is its place in a graphic object?

Every printed 'copy' contains drawings generated with a serial seed. Thus, each drawing will differ between them and from one edition to another. The cover is silk printed, and the pages are binds by kettle stitch.
