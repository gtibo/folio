Intimacy

An interactive poem composed of text image and sound. Intimacy explores the boundary between our usages with technology, ourselves and others. You can play and interact with it direcly in your web browser.

Brush your hand against the tender textures, try to rebuild something that's forever changed, confront the stares of the crowd ...

This project was entirely done through the html5 Canvas API, every shape is drawn on the fly and is sensible to the user’s interaction.
