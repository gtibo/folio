Digital Ghost

This application was thought for the 2021 open days of the HEAD. The final formes is an installation of multiples devices scattered across different places of the school.

Each device connected to the server can call others and get responses, like frogs chanting in a pond. Thus, merging the physical space into an interconnected and united one. This project explores the boundaries and relations we maintain with our devices and technology. Should we give personalities to AI? How data flows between humans, devices, and servers?

The program takes the shape of a web application displaying colorful characters staring the viewer. This choice reflects the trend of the personal assistant. Thus, bringing the machine closer to the user by making it look friendlier. The displayed characters are inspired by folklore and creatures like the Yamabiko yokai.

At the start of the installation, the devices only communicate with a mix of natural and learned sounds but slowly incorporate mimicked sounds and voices they heard. Those sounds are mixed through effects and sampler. This creates a disturbing chorus composed of mixed voices and sounds. Devices inherit properties, each with an individual level. Characteristics like shyness determine the behavior of the device, and its will to communicate with others.
